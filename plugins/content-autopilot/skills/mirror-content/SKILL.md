# Mirror Content

The most shared content doesn't inform — it says what the reader is already thinking.

## When to Activate

- User says `/mirror` or `/mirror {topic}`
- User asks "what does my audience really feel?"
- User wants to create highly resonant, shareable content

## Commands

### `/mirror {topic}` — Generate mirror content for a topic
### `/mirror scan` — Scan for unspoken audience sentiments
### `/mirror {file}` — Enhance existing content with mirror elements

## Workflow

### Step 1: Mine Unspoken Sentiments

Analyze comment-insights, niche-monitor, and audience-psychograph data:

```
Unspoken Audience Sentiments: "{topic}"
============================================

What they SAY: "AI tools are useful"
What they FEEL: "I'm afraid of being left behind if I don't learn AI"

What they SAY: "I want to automate my workflow"
What they FEEL: "I'm exhausted and desperate for relief, not just efficiency"

What they SAY: "Which tool is best?"
What they FEEL: "I'm overwhelmed by choices and afraid of picking wrong"

Underlying emotions:
  1. Fear of irrelevance
  2. Decision fatigue
  3. Imposter syndrome about new technology
  4. Desire for permission to not know everything
```

### Step 2: Generate Mirror Content

```
Mirror Content: "{title that articulates the unspoken}"

Hook: "{the sentence they've been thinking but couldn't say}"
  Example: "You don't actually want another AI tool. You want permission to not be an expert at everything."

Body: {Validates their feeling → explains why it's normal → provides relief + path forward}

Why this works:
  - Reader thinks "finally, someone gets it"
  - Emotional resonance > information value for sharing
  - Comments will be "I needed to hear this"
```

### Step 3: Mirror Enhancement

For existing content, add mirror elements:
```
Original: "Here are 5 AI tools for productivity"
Mirror-enhanced: "If you're overwhelmed by AI tools (you're not alone — 78% of professionals feel the same), here are the only 5 that actually matter"

The mirror element: acknowledging the overwhelm before providing the solution.
```

## Quality Gate

- [ ] Sentiments based on actual audience signals (not projected)
- [ ] Content validates feelings without being manipulative
- [ ] Mirror elements feel genuine (not psychologically exploitative)
- [ ] Actionable path provided after emotional resonance
- [ ] Balance between emotion and information maintained
