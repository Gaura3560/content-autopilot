# Content Brief Generator

Delegate content creation without losing quality — briefs so clear anyone can execute them.

## When to Activate

- User says `/brief {topic}` or `/content-brief {topic}`
- User asks "create a brief for a writer"
- User asks "I want to outsource this article"
- User wants to delegate content creation

## Prerequisites

- `~/.content-autopilot/profile.json` must exist
- Optional: brand-voice.json, content-dna.json, algorithm-guide.json

## Commands

### `/brief {topic}` — Generate a complete content brief
### `/brief {topic} {platform}` — Brief for a specific platform
### `/brief template` — Show the brief template (empty)

## Workflow

### Step 1: Gather Context

Compile from all available data:
- Profile: theme, audience, style, brand
- Brand voice: tone, vocabulary, personality (from brand-voice.json)
- Content DNA: what works for this account (from content-dna.json)
- Algorithm: current platform optimization tips (from algorithm-guide.json)
- SEO: target keywords (from seo research)

### Step 2: Generate Brief

```markdown
# Content Brief

## Overview
- **Topic**: {topic}
- **Platform**: {platform}
- **Format**: {article / thread / carousel / video script}
- **Target length**: {range}
- **Funnel stage**: {TOFU / MOFU / BOFU}
- **Due date**: {if specified}
- **Priority**: {high / medium / low}

## Target Audience
- **Who**: {audience description from profile}
- **Knowledge level**: {beginner / intermediate / advanced}
- **Pain point this addresses**: {specific problem}
- **Desired outcome after reading**: {what they should know/do/feel}

## Content Direction
- **Angle**: {specific angle/perspective to take}
- **Key message**: {the one thing the reader should remember}
- **Thesis**: {the argument or claim being made}
- **Unique value**: {what makes this different from existing content on this topic}

## Research & Sources
- **Required research areas**: {list of topics to research}
- **Suggested sources**: {URLs, publications, data sources}
- **Key data points to include**: {specific stats or facts}
- **Competitor articles to differentiate from**: {URLs}

## Structure
1. **Opening/Hook**: {specific hook type and direction}
   Example: "{sample opening line}"
2. **Section 1**: {heading + what to cover}
3. **Section 2**: {heading + what to cover}
4. **Section 3**: {heading + what to cover}
5. **Conclusion**: {how to wrap up}
6. **CTA**: {specific call to action}

## Brand Voice Guidelines
- **Tone**: {tone description}
- **Do**: {list of voice dos}
- **Don't**: {list of voice don'ts}
- **Signature phrases**: {phrases to use}
- **Vocabulary level**: {simple / accessible / technical}
- **Emoji usage**: {none / minimal / moderate}
- **Example of on-brand writing**: "{example sentence}"
- **Example of off-brand writing**: "{example sentence}"

## SEO Requirements (if note)
- **Primary keyword**: {keyword} — must appear in title + first paragraph
- **Secondary keywords**: {list} — use naturally in H2 headings
- **Meta description**: {suggested excerpt, max 120 chars}

## Platform Specifications
{Platform-specific requirements — length, format, hashtags, etc.}

## Title Requirements
- **Must use**: {title logic combos from content-dna}
- **Character limit**: {platform-specific}
- **Title candidates to consider**: {3 suggestions}

## Quality Criteria (Acceptance Checklist)
- [ ] Matches brand voice (check against guidelines above)
- [ ] Hook is compelling (would you stop scrolling?)
- [ ] Every paragraph earns its place (no filler)
- [ ] Data/examples are specific (not generic)
- [ ] CTA is clear and matches funnel stage
- [ ] SEO keywords included naturally
- [ ] Within length range
- [ ] Original content (not rewritten from a single source)

## Reference Materials
- **Brand voice guide**: {link to brand-voice-guide.md}
- **Best performing similar content**: {link to example}
- **Swipe file reference**: {relevant swipe entry}

---
Brief generated by Content Autopilot
Account: {name} | Theme: {theme.main}
```

### Step 3: Save Brief

Save to `~/Desktop/content-autopilot-output/`:
```
brief_{topic_slug}_{date}.md
```

## Integration with Other Skills

- **brand-voice**: Voice guidelines pulled from brand-voice.json
- **content-dna**: Title logic preferences and format recommendations
- **seo-optimizer**: Keyword research included in brief
- **algorithm-guide**: Platform optimization tips included
- **content-grader**: Quality criteria section maps to grader dimensions

## Quality Gate

- [ ] Brief is complete enough for a stranger to write from
- [ ] Voice guidelines are specific (not "write well")
- [ ] Structure is detailed but allows creative freedom
- [ ] SEO requirements are included where relevant
- [ ] Acceptance checklist is binary (pass/fail)
- [ ] Reference materials are linked and accessible
